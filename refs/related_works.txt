		- [10]: F.Naima et al. trích xuất R location và RR interval sử dụng Db4 discrete wavelet transform. Phân lớp bằng NN đạt kết quả. Sensitivity, specificity và accuracy là 90, 90 và 95.

		- [14]: R peak, RRt interval (current RR interval at t time), RRt+1 interval (next RR interval at t+1 time), QRSh (QRS complex height), and QRS width được sử dụng. Sensitivity và specificity đạt được sử dụng PSO-RBFNN là 96.251 % và 99.104%

- ECG Heartbeat Classification: A Deep Transferable Representation (Main ref)
	- Preprocessing:
		+ Chỉ xài ECG Lead II
		+ 10s window beat
		+ Normalizing value về 0 - 1
		+ Finding the set of all local maximums based on zero crossings of the first derivative
		+ Finding the set of ECG R-peak candidates by applying a threshold of 0.9 on the normalized value of the local maximums
		+ Finding the median of R-R time intervals as the nominal heartbeat period of that window (T).
		+  For each R-peak, selecting a signal part with the length equal to 1.2T
		+ Padding each selected part with zeros to make its length equal to a predefined fixed length
